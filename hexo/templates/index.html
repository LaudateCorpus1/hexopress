HexoPress
A tiny blogging platform.
Write your posts in Google Docs.
Put them under a folder in your Google Drive.
Publish a blog out of them.
Get Started
How?
Once you sign up, we'll drop a folder named hexopress into your Google Drive. From that point onwards, every Google Docs file you put in that folder will be compiled into blog posts for your freely hosted hexopress blog.
Get started now.
Why?
The project is based off Octopress. Octopress is for hackers with terminals and gits. Hexopress takes the elegance of Octopress and combines it with the beautiful, powerful & easily accessible Google Docs. Not everytime you have a brilliant idea, you will have access to a markdown editor or a terminal. With Google Docs you can now write anywhere, on any device and sync them to your blog in a click!
