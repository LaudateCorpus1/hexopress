Start Blogging Now!
How?
Create a folder named 'blog' in your google drive. That's all! Every google doc from the folder, will be a post in your HexoPress blog.
Get started now.
Why?
The project is based off Octopress. Octopress is for hackers with terminals and gits. Hexopress takes the elegance of Octopress and combines it with the beautiful, powerful & easily accessible Google Docs. Not everytime you have a brilliant idea, you have access to a markdown editor or a terminal. With Google Docs you can now write anywhere, on any device and still, get a blog out of them in a click!
